Rules

Welcome to Tic-Tac-Toe: 2-Player

The Objective of the game is match either Three "X's" or three "O's" to Win!
You may do so Horizontally ---, Vertically |||, or Diagonally \ or /

Players alternate turns selecting a tile in the Game Board to place in any space that they choose
If a player should win, their score will increase and the board will reset for the next round!
